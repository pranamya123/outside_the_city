Quick answers

Before you write.

Who do you serve?+

Formerly gang-involved and previously incarcerated men and women in Orange, San Diego, and LA counties — and we travel beyond when trips call for it.

Is there a cost for participants?+

No. Every outing — gear, food, transport, guides — is free, funded by donors and corporate partners.

How fast do you reply?+

General & volunteer: within 2 business days. Corporate & product donations: same week. Press: same day during active outings.

Can I volunteer without outdoor experience?+

Yes — several of our roles (Team Builders, Seniors) rely more on life experience and presence than on trail skills.

Are donations tax-deductible?+

Yes. We operate under Teen Health, Inc., a 501(c)(3). EIN 87-4628884.
