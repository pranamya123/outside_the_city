Transformation is our goal.

Our mission

To give formerly gang-involved and previously incarcerated men and women the chance to rewrite their stories — one life-changing experience at a time.

Our outdoor programs immerse participants in challenges that push limits, build confidence, and ignite renewed purpose. For many, it's their first time hiking rugged trails, kayaking open waters, navigating overland 4×4 terrain, or sleeping under a star-filled sky far from city lights.

Beyond adventure, we provide mentorship, guidance, and support — helping participants translate the courage and skills they gain outdoors into lasting change. They return with more than memories. They return with hope, determination, and a vision for a better future.

Our outings build mental toughness, leadership, teamwork, stress management, and practical skills like navigation and endurance — alongside access to environments most participants have never imagined they belonged in.

Why recreation?

Not escape.
Reintegration.

For many who've faced incarceration, these moments represent far more than a break from routine. They're what we call —

A coined phrase
"Life Resume Experiences."

Milestones that help redefine identity, build confidence, and reconnect with the world in positive, transformative ways. They become part of a new personal narrative — an expanded life resume that goes beyond what's written on paper.

Standing at the edge of a canyon. Kayaking a still bay at sunrise. Sharing stories around a fire in a desert camp. Each moment builds not just a memory, but a sense of self-worth and direction.

We capture every outing through photography, video, and drone footage — honoring the resilience and transformation, and celebrating the milestones.
