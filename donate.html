Product donations

New or used gear.
All of it helps.

Outdoor adventure gear, demo equipment, and bicycles all help bring first-time experiences to our participants. A used mountain bike can inspire someone to explore the world.

Camping & shelter

  • Tents (2p–6p)
  • Sleeping bags
  • Sleeping pads
  • Camp stoves & cookware

Hiking & apparel

  • Hiking boots / trail shoes
  • Backpacks (day + multi-day)
  • Rain shells & layers
  • Hats, gloves, headlamps

Water & wheels

  • Kayaks, paddles, PFDs
  • Stand-up paddle boards
  • Mountain bikes (any condition)
  • Bike helmets
